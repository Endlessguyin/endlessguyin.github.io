About This Game

Yohoho.io is a fast-paced, pirate-themed battle royale game where players take on the role of swashbuckling pirates fighting for dominance on a shrinking island. Armed with cutlasses, players must defeat opponents, collect gold, and grow in size and power. The more enemies you take down, the stronger you become, allowing you to unleash even more powerful attacks.
