About This Game

Optric is a puzzle-platformer game where players control a small orb of light navigating through a series of challenging levels. The game revolves around manipulating light-based mechanics to solve puzzles, activate platforms, and progress through increasingly complex stages. Players must carefully plan their movements, use reflection and refraction principles, and avoid obstacles to reach the goal. The minimalist yet visually engaging design, combined with clever level design, makes it an enjoyable experience for those who love brain-teasing challenges.
