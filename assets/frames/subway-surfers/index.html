About This Game

Subway Surfers is the ultimate fast‑paced endless runner that catapults you into a vibrant, urban playground. Strap on your sneakers and race along neon‑lit subway tracks, weaving through oncoming trains, hurdling barriers, and pulling off gravity‑defying flips on your trusty hoverboard. Snag dazzling coins, magnets and power‑boosters to unlock a colorful roster of charismatic characters—each with their own signature style. Compete in weekly challenges, globe‑trot through exotic cities, customize your gear, and chase that perfect run in this nonstop rush of adrenaline and fun!
