About This Game

Set off on an extraordinary survival journey across floating islands in a vast 3D world, each rich with valuable resources. Discover stunning landscapes, fight fierce monsters to become stronger, and trade with villagers for essential supplies. Grow crops, raise animals, and secure a steady source of food and materials. The skies are filled with islands, each hiding secrets and challenges. Explore, survive, craft, and conquer in this thrilling adventure!
