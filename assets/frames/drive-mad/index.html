About This Game

Drive Mad is a high‑octane arcade racer that throws you into the driver’s seat of souped‑up muscle cars and futuristic speedsters, burning rubber across neon‑soaked cityscapes and desolate desert highways. Slam the pedal to the metal as you drift around hairpin turns, unleash nitro boosts to rocket past rivals, and pull off jaw‑dropping stunts over ramps and wreckage. Evade relentless cops in pulse‑pounding pursuit modes, collect cash and performance parts to tweak your ride’s engine, handling, and style, then challenge friends on worldwide leaderboards. With dynamic weather, day‑night cycles, and ever‑evolving race events, Drive Mad delivers nonstop thrills for gearheads craving that next epic rush!
